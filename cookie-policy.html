Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Northshore Press LLC ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our DrumDash website and game. We are committed to being transparent about how we use cookies and other tracking technologies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience. There are two main types of cookies:

  • Session cookies: These are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the game and website to function properly. They include:

  • Session management cookies that keep you logged in
  • Security cookies that detect suspicious activity
  • Preference cookies that remember your game settings

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our website and game. This helps us improve user experience and identify areas for enhancement. These cookies are provided by:

  • Cloudflare Analytics: Helps us understand website traffic and performance
  • Unity Analytics: Provides insights into gameplay behavior and user engagement

Advertising Cookies

We use advertising cookies to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. These include:

  • Unity Ads: Displays targeted advertisements within the game
  • Third-party ad network cookies that help deliver personalized content

4. Third-Party Cookies

We allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device for the purposes outlined in this policy. These third parties include:

  • Cloudflare: For website protection and analytics
  • Unity Technologies: For analytics and advertising
  • Ad Networks: For serving targeted advertisements

5. Your Cookie Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Allow cookies from specific websites
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Delete existing cookies

To manage your cookie preferences, visit your browser's help section or privacy settings. You can also manage your privacy settings through our Privacy Settings page.

6. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other local storage technologies such as:

  • Local Storage: Stores game progress and settings on your device
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage for your current session
  • IndexedDB: Allows offline gameplay functionality

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Currently, there is no industry standard for recognizing DNT signals. We do not currently respond to DNT browser signals, but you can control cookies through your browser settings and our Privacy Settings page.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the website and game means you accept the updated Cookie Policy.
